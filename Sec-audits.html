Security Audits & Compliance Services

Professional cybersecurity assessments and NIST framework compliance

Service Overview

I provide comprehensive security audit services to help organizations strengthen their cybersecurity posture and achieve compliance with industry standards. My methodology is grounded in the NIST Cybersecurity Framework, delivering structured assessments of security controls, risk management practices, and organizational resilience.

My approach accommodates organizations with varying levels of technical maturity, translating complex security concepts into actionable business guidance. I work closely with leadership teams to ensure security improvements align with operational requirements and budget constraints while maintaining effective risk mitigation.

Security and privacy controls for federal information systems and organizations, applicable to private sector risk management.

International standards for information security management systems and implementation guidance.

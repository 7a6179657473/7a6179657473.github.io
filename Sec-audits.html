Security Audits & NIST Standards

Professional experience in conducting security audits and compliance assessments

Overview

I have conducted security audits for organizations seeking to strengthen their cybersecurity posture and ensure compliance with industry standards. My approach is rooted in the NIST Cybersecurity Framework, providing a structured and comprehensive assessment of security controls and risk management practices.

In addition to technical assessments, I have provided tailored guidance and assistance to business owners with varying levels of computer and internet literacy. This includes explaining complex security concepts in accessible terms, helping clients understand risks relevant to their operations, and supporting them in adopting best practices regardless of their technical background.

Security Audit Experience

  • Performed end-to-end security audits for small and medium businesses
  • Assessed technical and administrative controls for compliance with NIST SP 800-53 and NIST CSF
  • Identified vulnerabilities in network, endpoint, and cloud environments
  • Developed actionable remediation plans and presented findings to leadership
  • Collaborated with IT teams to implement security improvements
  • Provided guidance on policy development and incident response planning

Generalized Security Audit Process (NIST-Based)

  • Preparation: Define audit scope, objectives, and gather relevant documentation
  • Control Identification: Map organizational controls to NIST standards (e.g., SP 800-53, CSF)
  • Assessment: Evaluate the effectiveness of controls through interviews, technical testing, and document review
  • Gap Analysis: Identify deficiencies and areas of non-compliance
  • Reporting: Document findings, risk levels, and provide prioritized recommendations
  • Remediation & Follow-up: Support remediation efforts and verify implementation of corrective actions

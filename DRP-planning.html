Disaster Recovery Planning

Business continuity and disaster recovery services for organizations

Service Overview

I provide comprehensive disaster recovery planning (DRP) and business continuity services tailored for small to medium organizations. My methodology ensures critical business operations can be restored quickly and cost-effectively following disruptive incidents including cyberattacks, infrastructure failures, natural disasters, and other operational disruptions.

Working collaboratively with leadership teams and key personnel, I develop practical, actionable recovery plans that align with organizational capabilities, budget constraints, and regulatory requirements while maintaining focus on operational resilience and business continuity.

DRP Development Methodology

1

Assessment & Planning

Comprehensive analysis of organizational structure, critical functions, and existing recovery capabilities.

  • Stakeholder interviews and requirement gathering
  • Current state assessment of backup and recovery systems
  • Regulatory and compliance requirement analysis
  • Project scope definition and timeline establishment
2

Business Impact Analysis

Detailed evaluation of critical business functions, dependencies, and potential impact of various disruption scenarios.

  • Critical business function identification and mapping
  • System interdependency analysis and documentation
  • RTO/RPO determination for each critical function
  • Financial impact modeling and cost-benefit analysis
3

Risk Assessment & Strategy

Identification and evaluation of potential threats with development of appropriate mitigation and recovery strategies.

  • Threat landscape analysis and risk quantification
  • Vulnerability assessment and gap analysis
  • Recovery strategy selection and validation
  • Resource requirement planning and budgeting
4

Implementation & Testing

Development of detailed recovery procedures, staff training, and comprehensive testing to validate plan effectiveness.

  • Detailed recovery procedure documentation
  • Emergency response team training and certification
  • Tabletop exercises and full-scale testing
  • Plan maintenance and continuous improvement processes

Industry Best Practices & Standards

ISO 22301 Business Continuity

International standard for business continuity management systems, providing framework for planning, establishing, implementing, and continually improving business continuity management.

NIST SP 800-34 Contingency Planning

Federal guidelines for information technology contingency planning, applicable to private sector disaster recovery and business continuity planning.

FEMA Business Continuity Guidelines

Federal Emergency Management Agency best practices for business continuity planning and emergency preparedness for organizations of all sizes.

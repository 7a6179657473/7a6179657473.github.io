About Me

Cybersecurity Consultant | Penetration Tester | Security Enthusiast

Who I Am

I am a cybersecurity professional with a unique blend of experiences ranging from military service, freelance consulting & my hobbies. My journey began in the United States Marine Corps, where I honed my skills in administration, compliance, and leadership. This foundation has propelled me into a career focused on uncovering vulnerabilities and empowering organizations to enhance their defenses against various threats.

Outside of work, I enjoy exploring and experimenting with new technologies, often delving into the intricacies of devices and gadgets to understand their capabilities. This includes challenging myself with rooting personal devices as a learning exercise. Additionally, I find balance through outdoor activities such as rucking and hiking, which allow me to connect with nature while pursuing personal fitness goals.

Experience

My professional experience includes:

  • Plant Administrator: Managing operational data, budget development, and procurement at Mutual Materials.
  • IT Security Consultant: Performing security control assessments and vulnerability analyses for non-profit organizations.
  • Cybersecurity Consultant: Conducting audits, emplore disaster recovery planning, and educate organizational leadership on best practices.
  • Administrative Roles in the Marine Corps: Managing personnel, developing SOPs, and ensuring compliance with USMC regulations.

Additionally, I have actively participated in bug bounty programs, working to uncover vulnerabilities in real-world applications and contributing to safer online environments. My succesfull contributations has affected State, Federal, Education & Consumer oragnizations.

Education & Certifications

I hold an Associate of Applied Science in Cybersecurity Penetration Testing from Mt. Hood Community College. My education has equipped me with technical expertise in network security, penetration testing, and compliance frameworks.

I am continuously pursuing certifications and training to stay afloat of industry advancements.

Core Beliefs

I believe in fostering a educated driven mindset in every organization I collaborate with. By providing actionable real world insights and practical solutions, I strive to aassist teams to proactively identify risks and build resiliency.

Every interaction, whether a brief consultation or a long-term project, is an opportunity to leave a worthwhile positive impact.

Personal Interests

When I am not immersed in the world of cybersecurity, I enjoy failing through bug bounties, sharing insights and technical knowledge with friends and family, often enthusiastically providing detailed explanations without prior warning, and exploring the intersections of technology and online human/Bot behavior.

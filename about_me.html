About Me

Cybersecurity Consultant | Auditor | Bug Bounty Hunter | Former Marine

Who I Am

I am a dedicated cybersecurity professional with a unique blend of experiences ranging from military service to freelance consulting. My journey began in the United States Marine Corps, where I honed my skills in administration, compliance, and leadership. This foundation has propelled me into a career focused on uncovering vulnerabilities and empowering organizations to fortify their defenses against modern threats.

Outside of work, I am a lifelong learner passionate about staying ahead in the ever-evolving field of cybersecurity. I am driven by curiosity and the belief that technology can be a force for good when secured effectively.

Experience

My professional experience includes:

  • Plant Administrator: Managing operational data, budget development, and procurement at Mutual Materials.
  • Cybersecurity Consultant: Conducting audits, disaster recovery planning, and compliance evaluations for small to medium-sized businesses.
  • IT Security Consultant: Performing security control assessments and vulnerability analyses for non-profit organizations.
  • Administrative Roles in the Marine Corps: Leading personnel, developing SOPs, and ensuring compliance with federal regulations.

Additionally, I have actively participated in bug bounty programs, uncovering vulnerabilities in real-world applications and contributing to safer online environments.

Education & Certifications

I hold an Associate of Applied Science in Cybersecurity Penetration Testing from Mt. Hood Community College. My education has equipped me with technical expertise in network security, penetration testing, and compliance frameworks.

I am continuously pursuing certifications and training to stay abreast of industry advancements.

Core Beliefs

I believe in fostering a security-first mindset in every organization I collaborate with. By providing actionable insights and practical solutions, I strive to empower teams to proactively manage risks and build resilient systems.

Every interaction, whether a brief consultation or a long-term project, is an opportunity to leave a lasting positive impact.

Personal Interests

When I am not immersed in the world of cybersecurity, I enjoy problem-solving through bug bounties, mentoring aspiring professionals, and exploring the intersections of technology and human behavior. My time in the Marine Corps has instilled a sense of discipline and a commitment to excellence that I carry into every aspect of my life.
